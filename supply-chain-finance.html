Supply chain finance

Exponent’s Supply Chain Finance, also known as Reverse Factoring or Supplier Finance, allows you to accelerate cash flow while it ensures that the suppliers get their payment on time by verified investors. In simple terms, SCF enables the Buyer to pay later where the suppliers get their payments earlier, which improves both ends without disturbing their financial position.
